Past Events

Evening with Industry 2020

Thank you to everyone who attended SWE SJSU’s Evening with Industry! Throughout the evening, students were able to speak with representatives (recruiters, engineers, and past interns) from various companies about current openings, were able to share their resumes, and were able to connect with HR outside of online applications!

Women in Enginering 2019

The world’s largest conference and career fair for women in engineering and technology where you can connect with thousands who share your engineering outlook.

Hack 4 Safety

In recognition of October being Domestic Violence Awareness Month, Hack 4 Safety is an one-day Hackathon where the focus will be on the prevention of domestic violence—specifically a phone app for early detection of domestic violence for teens.

Pumpkin Painting Social

SWEsters came together to carve and paint pumpkins for Halloween.

Yelp Company Tour

Another successful company tour! This time at the Yelp HQ in San Francisco. Attendees listened to Yelp engineers talk about their software design process and had the opportunity to ask questions.

Evening with Industry 2.0

EWI was loads of fun! Thank you to everyone that attended. Hope you were able to find answers to your burning questions about working as a professional engineer.
